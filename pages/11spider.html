Walking into Spider Webs

Scream Level: 🕷️😱🕷️

You’re just out for a peaceful stroll when BAM—face full of spider web and an immediate descent into ninja flailing. That feeling? We made it into dessert.


"Web of Lies" is a deceptively innocent vanilla scream base, tangled with sticky marshmallow webs and surprise crunchy "spider" clusters (they’re just chocolate… probably). It’s confusing, chaotic, and leaves you questioning your life choices—just like real webs in real life. Guaranteed to make you swipe at your face for no reason. Repeatedly.
