iScream


Traffic when you're already late

Scream Level: 😡😡😡

You left late. But you had a plan. You’d make up time. You’d hit all the green lights. The universe just had to cooperate. Then—red brake lights until the horizon and the creeping realization that you're now part of the very problem you screamed about five minutes ago.


Enter Gridlock Rocky Road, the flavor of inevitability and silent screaming. It starts with a rich chocolate base—heavy, intense, and full of regret. Inside are chunks of “bad decision” brownie, swirls of slow-moving fudge, and crushed peanuts of anxiety—all jammed together like angry commuters in a six-lane bottleneck. Threaded throughout is a red “Road Rage Ripple,” a spicy cinnamon syrup that hits hard and lingers, much like your now-ruined ETA. Each spoonful delivers a powerful mix of chocolatey despair and marshmallowy denial, because hey—at least you thought you could get there on time. Served with a complimentary honk and a sense that someone, somewhere, is definitely driving 15 miles under the limit just to spite you. Best consumed with your hazard lights on and a podcast you’re too mad to pay attention to.
